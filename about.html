Our Story

Where It All
Began

Ceremony Squad was founded in 2022 by a small group of passionate event designers who shared one conviction: that a truly memorable celebration is never accidental. It is imagined, shaped, and brought to life — detail by deliberate detail.

What started as late-night sketches and weekend pop-up ceremonies has grown into one of India's most sought-after luxury event houses. We've decorated palace courtyards in Rajasthan, transformed rooftops in Mumbai, and sent guests home with memories that last a lifetime.

Our work is guided by three principles — Vision, Restraint, and Warmth. We believe elegance is never loud, and that the quietest details carry the most meaning.

2022

The Spark

Three friends with a shared obsession — beautiful events — founded Ceremony Squad in a small studio in Lajpat Nagar, Delhi. Our very first wedding had 80 guests and zero mistakes.

2023

Expansion & Recognition

We crossed 100 events and expanded to Mumbai and Jaipur. Our signature "Floating Petal Ceremony" installation went viral on social media, putting us on the national map.

Meet the Visionary

Aanya Mehta

"I believe a celebration is the most honest portrait of who you are. My job is to make sure that portrait is breathtaking."

Aanya Mehta built Ceremony Squad from a single notebook of ideas and an unshakeable belief that every event could be a work of art. With a background in interior architecture and a decade spent studying luxury hospitality in London and Milan, she returned to India with a singular vision: to bring world-class event craft to celebrations that deserve nothing less.

Her design philosophy blends tactile richness with visual restraint — rooms that feel both opulent and intimate, moments that feel both grand and personal. She leads every major event personally, from the first concept sketch to the final farewell.
